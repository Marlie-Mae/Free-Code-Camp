Elizabeth II

Queen of the United Kingdom and other Commonwealth realms

Here's a time line of HM Queen Elizabth II:

  • 1926 - Born in Mayfair, London, as the first child of the Duke and Duchess of York.
  • 1936 - Her father becomes King George VI following the abdication of his brother, King Edward VIII.
  • 1945 - Serves in the Auxiliary Territorial Service during World War II, becoming the first female member of the royal family to join the armed forces.
  • 1947 - Marries Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh, in a grand ceremony at Westminster Abbey.
  • 1952 - Becomes Queen Elizabeth II at the age of 25 after the death of her father.
  • 1953 - Coronation ceremony takes place at Westminster Abbey, broadcast on television for the first time.
  • 1977 - Celebrates her Silver Jubilee, marking 25 years on the throne.
  • 2002 - Commemorates her Golden Jubilee, celebrating 50 years as queen.
  • 2012 - Celebrates her Diamond Jubilee, marking 60 years on the throne with events across the UK and Commonwealth.
  • 2022 - Celebrates her Platinum Jubilee, the first British monarch to reach 70 years on the throne.
  • 2022 - Passes away on September 8, marking the end of her historic reign.

"Her Majesty's reign was marked by extraordinary change, including the transformation of the British Empire into the Commonwealth, and she remained a symbol of continuity and stability."

-- Official Website of the British Royal Family
